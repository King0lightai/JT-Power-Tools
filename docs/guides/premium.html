License Verification

How Verification Works

When you activate your license:

  1. Extension sends your license key to Gumroad's API
  2. Gumroad verifies the key is valid and active
  3. Response confirms license status
  4. Extension stores verification locally
  5. Premium features are unlocked

Privacy & Security

  • Only License Key Transmitted - No personal data or JobTread information is sent
  • Secure Connection - HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Gumroad Privacy - Gumroad's privacy policy applies: gumroad.com/privacy
  • No Tracking - We don't track your usage or collect analytics

Troubleshooting License Issues

License Key Not Working

Solution:

  • Double-check you copied the entire key (no extra spaces)
  • Verify you subscribed from the correct Gumroad page
  • Check if your payment was successful
  • Try copying and pasting the key again
  • Contact Gumroad support if payment issues

"Invalid License" Error

Solution:

  • Ensure you're entering the license key, not order number
  • Check for typos or missing characters
  • Verify the license hasn't been refunded or disputed
  • Try activating on a different browser to test

"Connection Error" During Activation

Solution:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify Gumroad.com is accessible (not blocked by firewall)
  • Try again in a few minutes (could be temporary Gumroad issue)
  • Check if corporate firewall is blocking api.gumroad.com

Premium Features Not Working After Activation

Solution:

  • Verify you see "License Active" in popup
  • Check that premium feature toggles are turned ON
  • Refresh the JobTread page
  • Try disabling and re-enabling the feature
  • Deactivate and reactivate your license

License Shows Active But Features Locked

Solution:

  • Close and reopen the extension popup
  • Refresh the JobTread page
  • Check browser console (F12) for errors
  • Try clicking the premium feature toggle off and on
  • Report the issue on GitHub with details

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the license a subscription?

Yes, all tiers are monthly subscriptions. Your subscription renews monthly and can be cancelled anytime through Gumroad.

What's the difference between tiers?

Each tier builds on the previous one. Essential adds productivity tools, Pro adds customization and power features, Power User adds API-powered features and priority support.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes! Start with any tier and upgrade anytime. Contact support to switch your subscription to a higher tier.

Can I use it on multiple computers?

Yes! Your license is per-company, not per-device or per-user. Install on all computers within your organization.

Can I share my license with my team?

Absolutely! All tiers are designed for company-wide sharing. One subscription covers your entire company ‚Äî share the license key with all employees.

What if I lose my license key?

Check your email from Gumroad. You can also log into your Gumroad account to view your purchases and license keys.

Do I need internet to use premium features?

Internet is required for initial activation and for API-powered features (Custom Field Filter). Other premium features work offline after activation.

Can I try premium features before subscribing?

Currently there's no free trial, but you can cancel your subscription anytime. If premium features don't meet your needs, cancel through Gumroad.

Will new features cost extra?

New features are added to the appropriate tier. Your subscription includes access to all features in your tier and below, including new ones as they're released.
