⚡ Quick Job Switcher

Essential Feature - Enhanced Job Navigation

Essential Feature

This feature requires an Essential license or higher. Learn how to activate your license.

Overview

Quick Job Switcher enhances JobTread's built-in job switcher (J+S) with two powerful improvements: keyboard-driven selection and a resizable sidebar that remembers your preferences. Search for a job, press Enter to select it and close the sidebar - all without touching your mouse.

What This Feature Adds

JobTread already has a job switcher that opens with the J+S keyboard shortcut. Quick Job Switcher enhances it with:

  • Enter to Select - Press Enter to select the highlighted job AND close the sidebar automatically
  • Resizable Sidebar - Drag the edge of the sidebar to make it wider or narrower
  • Size Memory - Your preferred sidebar width is saved and restored every time you open it

Getting Started

Prerequisites

  • JT Power Tools extension installed in Chrome
  • Active Essential license or higher
  • Access to JobTread

Enabling the Feature

  1. Click the JT Power Tools extension icon in your Chrome toolbar
  2. Locate the "Quick Job Switcher" toggle (marked with Essential badge)
  3. Toggle it ON - the switch will turn blue
  4. Navigate to any JobTread page
  5. Press J + S to open the job switcher

How to Use

Keyboard Selection

The main enhancement is the ability to select a job and close the sidebar with a single keystroke:

  1. Open the Switcher
    Press J + S (JobTread's built-in shortcut)
  2. Search for a Job
    Type to filter the job list - JobTread's search is automatically focused
  3. Press Enter
    Hit Enter to select the highlighted job AND close the sidebar
Before vs After

Without this feature: You search for a job, then have to click on it, and the sidebar stays open.
With this feature: Search, press Enter, done! The job is selected and the sidebar closes automatically.

Resizable Sidebar

Customize the sidebar width to fit your workflow:

  1. Open the Switcher
    Press J + S
  2. Find the Edge
    Hover your mouse over the left edge of the sidebar - your cursor will change to a resize cursor
  3. Drag to Resize
    Click and drag left to make it wider, or right to make it narrower
  4. Release
    Let go when you're happy with the size - it's automatically saved!

Size Memory

Your preferred sidebar width is remembered across sessions:

  • Resize the sidebar once to your preferred width
  • Close the sidebar
  • Next time you open it, it will be exactly the same width
  • Works across browser sessions - your preference persists

Keyboard Shortcuts

Shortcut Function Source
J + S Open job switcher JobTread (built-in)
Enter Select job and close sidebar JT Power Tools
Escape Close sidebar without selecting JobTread (built-in)
↑ ↓ Navigate through results JobTread (built-in)

Common Use Cases

Power User Workflow

For users who switch between jobs frequently:

  • Keep your hands on the keyboard the entire time
  • J+S → type search → Enter - all in one fluid motion
  • No need to reach for the mouse to click on the job

Wide Sidebar for Long Job Names

If your jobs have long names that get cut off:

  • Resize the sidebar wider to see full job names
  • Your wider preference is saved for next time
  • Easier to find the right job when names are visible

Narrow Sidebar to See More Content

If you want to see more of the main content while browsing jobs:

  • Resize the sidebar narrower
  • Still functional, just more compact
  • Great for smaller screens or split-screen workflows

Troubleshooting

Enter Key Not Working

Solution:

  • Verify Quick Job Switcher is enabled in the extension popup
  • Make sure a job is highlighted in the search results
  • Try refreshing the JobTread page
  • Check that your Essential license is active

Sidebar Size Not Saving

Solution:

  • Make sure the feature is enabled
  • Check that the extension has storage permissions
  • Try resizing again and waiting a moment before closing

Can't Resize Sidebar

Solution:

  • Make sure you're hovering over the left edge of the sidebar
  • Look for the resize cursor (horizontal double arrow)
  • The feature may need the page to be refreshed after enabling
